AERobot (Affordable Education Robot)

AERobot is a simple low-cost robot for introductory programming and robotics education. It provides a wide range of sensors and abilities, controlled by an intuitive graphical programming environment, all for a fraction of the cost of other educational robots.

Features:

  • Sensors provide abilities like obstacle detection and ranging, line-following, and light orientation, making it suitable for a variety of activities like maze-solving
  • Movement on flat surfaces
  • Multicolor LED
  • Low cost makes it feasible for all students to have, and personalize, their own robot
  • Plugs directly into USB port for programming and charging -- no separate cable required
  • Hardware and programming environment are both open-source

What's included

Each kit is complete with a robot chassis, materials to attach a "foot" (plastic pad, standoffs (2), bolts (2), nuts (2); takes 1 minute and requires no tools), and a rechargeable battery.
